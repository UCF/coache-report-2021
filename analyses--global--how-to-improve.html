This page contains COACHE's thematic analysis of the final open-ended question of the survey. We ask faculty to identify the one thing they feel their institutions could do to improve the workplace for faculty. COACHE reviews all comments, redacts any identifying information, and codes them thematically.

In Numbers

This chart displays the relative frequency of themes mentioned by your faculty in response to this question. Note that responses often touch upon multiple themes, so the total number of comments reported in this thematic summary will exceed the actual number of faculty who responded to this question.
