In this COACHE Chief Academic Officer Report, we define a "response rate" as the percent of all qualified subjects whose responses, following the data conditioning process, were deemed eligible to be included in this analysis. The nonrespondents include faculty who were "screened out" by the survey application or by later data cleaning processes. Therefore, the proportion of faculty who started the survey may be higher than the response rate reported here.
